Star Wars Battlefront II — current loadout & playstyle

Star Wars Battlefront II is one of my all-time favourite games to stream. I main the Officer class for its squad support and versatility — the ability to drop a battle command, set up a turret, and keep the squad alive while racking up eliminations is hard to beat.

On the hero side, I've spent serious time mastering both BB-8 and BB-9E. BB-8 is a fantastic light side disruptor — his cable spin and shock drum make him a nightmare in close quarters. BB-9E mirrors that on the dark side with his rolling charge and ability to disrupt enemy abilities at just the right moment.

Whether it's Galactic Assault, Supremacy, or Heroes vs. Villains, you'll usually find me somewhere in the mix causing chaos. Follow along on Twitch to catch live sessions or check out YouTube for highlights.
